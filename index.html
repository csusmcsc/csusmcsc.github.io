About

The CSUSM Cybersecurity Club is a student organization officially recognized by California State University San Marcos. We are a group of students at Cal State San Marcos that are passionate about learning more about cybersecurity. We seek to enhance our knowledge, skills, and to create a legacy of comradery that current and future CSUSM students can benefit from. We welcome any and all majors into our club and encourage students at surrounding local community colleges to join our Discord channel.

We enjoy participating in cyber defense and capture the flag competitions such as: WRCCDC, picoCTF, and NCL among others. We also host our very own capture the flag competition C0ug4rCTF that any CSUSM student can participate in.

Meeting times typically take place from noon to 1pm Tuesdays and Thursdays in Academic Hall, room 202. Fall 2024 meeting times are TBD.
